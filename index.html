Recipe 1. Samosa

  • Time: 1 Hour 10 Minute
  • Serves: 8 Samosa
Ingredients Quantity
maida / plain flour 2 cup
ajwain / carom seeds ¼ tsp
salt ½ tsp
oil ¼ cup
water ½ cup
cumin / jeera 1 tsp
coriander seeds, crushed ½ tsp
fennel / saunf ½ tsp
pinch hing / asafoetida ½ tsp
ginger, finely chopped 1 inch
chilli, finely chopped 1
peas / matar ½ cup
kashmiri red chilli powder ½ tsp
potato / aloo, boiled & mashed 4

STEPS TO MAKE SAMOSA:-

  1. pinch a ball sized dough and grease with oil.
  2. roll the dough into oval shape.
  3. now cut it horizontally, diving into 2 equal portions using a knife.
  4. grease with water and make cone.
  5. stuff 2 tbsp of prepared samosa masala into the cone.
  6. grease little water on the edges.
  7. close and seal tightly by pressing firmly.
  8. deep-fry the samosa on a low flame. alternatively bake at 180 degree celcius for 40 minutes.
  9. stir occasionally, frying the samosa on low flame for atleast 15 minutes.
  10. once the aloo samosa turns golden and crisp, drain off over kitchen paper.
  11. finally, enjoy aloo samosa with green chutney and tamarind chutney.

Recipe 2. Rumali Roti

  • Time: 50 Minute
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
maida / all-purpose flour / plain flour salt to taste 2 cups
oil 2 tsp
warm milk, add as required to knead dough ¾ cup
maida / all-purpose flour to dust ¼ cup
water 1 cup
salt 1 tsp

STEPS TO MAKE RUMALI ROTI-:-

RUMALI ROTI DOUGH RECIPE:

  1. first of all, in a large mixing bowl, take 2 cups of maida.
  2. add pinch salt and a teaspoon of oil.
  3. add warm milk little by little and start to knead.
  4. knead for atleast 15 minutes.
  5. finally, grease the dough with a tsp of oil to prevent from drying.
  6. in conclusion, cover with moist cloth and rest for atleast 20 minutes

RUMALI ROTI RECIPE:

  1. pinch a small sized ball dough, roll and flatten it. also dust with some plain flour.
  2. furthermore, roll it into a thin circle.
  3. also dust with some plain flour.
  4. roll as thin as possible.
  5. stretch little so that it become thin.
  6. roll the rumali roti carefully over the rolling pin

RUMALI ROTI RECIPE:

  1. take kadai or chapathi tawa and heat for 2 minutes.
  2. then reverse the kadai and heat further more a minute.sprinkle some salt water over kadai.
  3. sprinkle some salt water over kadai.
  4. now slowly unroll the rumali roti over hot kadai.
  5. after 30 seconds you can see bubbles forming over roti.
  6. so flip off and cook the other side.
  7. so flip off and cook the other side.
  8. once the golden brown spots start appearing, the roomali roti is done.
  9. serve roti immediately with curry of your choice.

Recipe 3.Tea(Chai)

  • Time: 15 Minute
  • Serves: 2-5
Ingredients Quantity
loose-leaf black tea 4 teaspoons
sticks (each about 2 1/2 in. long) 1 or 2 cinnamon
black cardamom* pods, 1 or 2
green cardamom pods, crushed with a rolling pin or mallet 2 or 4
slices fresh ginger 1 or 2 quarter-size
sugar 1 1/2 tablespoons
whole or low-fat milk 1 cup

STEPS TO MAKE RUMALI ROTI-:-

  1. Pour 2 cups water into a medium saucepan and add tea and spices. Bring to a boil over high heat and boil 2 minutes.
  2. Add sugar and milk and return to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and boil gently 2 minutes, stirring occasionally. Strain and pour into small cups or heatproof glasses.
